Campus life

School Library

Was established in 1982

The school library is full of wonderful books for students of all ages to read. Each year, we do a book fair. This is where students get to find new books to buy and take home to keep.

School Soccer feild

Was established in 1982

The school soccer field is a large field on a small hill made with real grass. It also has a 100 meter track to sprint on to practice in physical education. Student can play a big game of soccer in the soccer field with all their peers.

School achievements

Athletics Teams
Have participated and won athletics tournaments.

Every year, all private schools come together and compete against each other to play sports such as ultimate frisbee, soccer, hockey, basketball and much more. A student will have to make it into our team by attending the try-outs that are during their break time and after school. If they make it, they get to come with us to the tournament and help our team win.

Chess Team
Have participated and won chess tournaments.

Each year, all private schools come together and compete against each other to play chess. A student will have to make it into our team by attending the try-outs that are during their break time. If they make it, they get to come with us to the tournament.

News In Fern Hill School

  • Student have the option to attend school online or in-person

    During this time, we think that some parents are not comfortable with dropping off their child or children for their own safety. We completely understand this issue so we have decided to make it optional for student to attend school in-person or online. Just send the office an email stating that your child or children will not be attending school in-person. You will have to sent this email weekly. If you child or children are not able to attending school in person for one day, please let us know.

  • Students MUST WEAR MASKS AT ALL TIME

    As COVID-19 is a very dangerous illness, along with the varients, we are all required to wear masks at all time, even when students are outside. When they are eating a snack or lunch, they must physical distancing and should not talk. Let's help prevent the spead of the virus!
